Since [Vintage Story](https://www.vintagestory.at) is an in-development sandbox indie game with a wide scope, there's tons of stuff in it that doesn't work very well. Either due to an oversight, unimplemented feature, conscious balancing decision, or a combination of all three+, there are many things that I'd like to see fixed in VS. This is an attempt to catalogue them (or up to when I get bored of writing l o l).

---

## Sticks
Sticks are an essential resource in the early and mid-game, but they're very frustrating to gather whether you have shears or not.

### Early Game
Sticks are either very dangerous or very boring to collect despite being needed in large quantities for firing pottery. You either run through forests (bears â†’ bad idea) and grab dropped ones, or hold LMB for about 3 minutes per stack in a shrubland, which is mind-numbingly boring.

### Mid Game
Pretending trees are wooly sheep and shearing them in their entirety before felling them (maybe don't fell sheep) is just stupid, finicky, and not fun.

### Solution
Chopping trees is more fun than killing shrubs and shaving trees. Add a time-intensive manual method of turning logs into sticks, and consider making the pit kiln not require them.

---

## Pottery Firing
Pit kilns are fine early-game, but because of the aforementioned reasons (and a few more), they're very annoying to use in the mid-game, nor does it make sense that you have to stick to literal hole-in-ground infrastructure technology to make pottery before you reach the late iron age and have the menagerie of resources to make a beehive kiln.

### Solution
Add a mid-game kiln a la the bloomery (but reusable) that requires only one fuel type and is accessible in the bronze age.
